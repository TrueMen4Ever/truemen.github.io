Role Models

These men are the peak of mascullinity.

  • Muhammed
  • Jesus
  • Moses
  • Abraham
  • Noah

Prophet Muhammed (Peace be upon him)

"The strong man is the one who controls himself when angry."

He was the model of self-mastery, leading with justice, mercy, and unmatched discipline. He faced enemies, temptations, and leadership burdens with unwavering strength and gentleness.

Prophet Jesus (Peace be upon him)

"Indeed, I am the servant of Allah. He has given me the Scripture and made me a prophet."

He embodied purity, humility, and spiritual power, resisting worldly temptations while calling people to truth. He stood firm in the face of rejection and falsehood without seeking revenge.

Prophet Moses (Peace be upon him)

"O my Lord, expand for me my chest and ease my task for me."

Moses faced the tyrant Pharaoh with courage and conviction, despite his own fears and speech difficulty. He was a warrior-prophet who led his people with strength and persistence.

Prophet Abraham (Peace be upon him)

"Indeed, I have turned my face toward Him who created the heavens and the earth, inclining toward truth."

He was the father of prophets, willing to sacrifice everything—even his son—for God. His manhood was defined by pure tawheed (monotheism), courage, and unwavering submission.

Prophet Noah (Peace be upon him)

"O my people, I am to you a clear warner."

He preached for 950 years with patience and resilience despite being mocked and rejected. His strength was in enduring isolation for the sake of truth.




Frequently Asked Questions

Here you will find some information about manhood and this page.

What defines a true man?

Strength in character, not ego. He leads, protects, and lives with purpose and discipline.

How is masculinity connected to faith?

True masculinity submits to God—it’s humble, strong, and principled.

Is self-control necessary to be a man?

Yes. Without it, you're a slave to your desires.

Why is discipline important?

Discipline builds strength, focus, and long-term success.

Can I be masculine without religion?

Maybe—but faith grounds masculinity in something higher than pride or power.

How do I start becoming a real man?

Submit to God, control your desires, serve others, and live with purpose.
